Introduction

One of my favorite Hobbies is to go surfing. I got into surfing around my junior year of High school and I have been going as much as I can ever since then. My friends and I love to longboard and we go to beaches like Doheney, Blackies, and San Onofre. I also have 2 shortboards but I prefer to longboard because it is more chill and more of my friends can surf smaller waves opposed to bigger waves.
Another one of my hobbies is to play music. I took piano lessons for a big part of my childhood and I had to things like piano recitals and stuff. But during my freshman year of high school, I picked up playing guitar and I have been playing ever since. I love playing the guitar and I play for the worship team at my churches and I have spent a ton of money on guitars and guitar related things.
